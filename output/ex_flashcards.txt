

FLASHCARDS:
Shoot,The stem of a plant that is just beginning to grow; it connects the roots and the leaves and is the pathway for nutrients to move through the plant.
Buds,The developing part of a plant that will grow into leaves, additional stems, or flowers.
Terminal bud,Bud that forms at the tip of a stem.
Lateral bud,A bud that forms along the side of a stem.
Bulb,A large underground bud that can produce new plants.
Bud scales,Special leaves that protect lateral buds on certain plants.
Axillary bud,A bud that forms in the axil of a leaf, which can develop into a lateral branch or flower.
Node,The point on a stem where a leaf is attached.
Internode,The section of a stem between two nodes.
